Welcome hrb repo pages

Hrb repositories are repositories built and managed by me and for my own purposes. I use them to distribute packages not availabe in CentOS or RepoForge. Packages are provided "as is" with no express or implied warranty for accuracy or accessibility.

Repositories

Every repository has an installer package: hrb-{fw,kernel,ssh,tls}-release.version.rpm and testing section. Debuginfo packages are available too.
